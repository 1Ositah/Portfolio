Hello, I'm Angie!


I’ve traveled the world in search of purpose, and every path has led me back to the same conclusion: visual communication is my calling.
I was born in Bogotá, Colombia, and grew up drawing my favorite things and collecting teddy bears, a passion I still carry with me today. From an early age, I was drawn to expressing ideas visually, finding meaning in images where words often fell short. That instinct naturally evolved into a passion for branding and identity design.
Helping people shape a visual identity that truly represents who they are and watching it come to life is one of the most rewarding parts of my work. I’m driven by the challenge of turning ideas into something tangible,
something that connects.
I hold a bachelor's degree in communication studies from Temple University Japan, where I developed a deeper understanding of how messages move people and shape cultures.
Looking ahead, I aim to continue exploring new places and perspectives that expand my creative practice and push my work in graphic design even further.
